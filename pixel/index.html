pixelThing v0.7

Use the Pixel Editor to send pixel graphics to the shared Pixel Wall. It works best if you run the editor on your mobile phone and the wall on your computer.

  • Open the Pixel Editor
  • Open the Pixel Wall

Open the Pixel Wall and scan the QR code displayed to open the Pixel Editor on other devices.

Remember, other people using the Pixel Wall at the same time will be able to see your creations too!
